Pray with Us

Pray for God’s love to pour out—Middle East

Unrest throughout the Middle East is building. All those living in the region know the pain of loss and constant chaos. The Scriptures say, “Pray for peace in Jerusalem: ‘May those who love you find peace. May there be peace within your walls. May there be safety in your great buildings …’” (Psalm 122:6 ERV). Pray that God will use Bible League and others to pour out His love and encourage His people as He causes great good to come out of the evils spreading across this region.

Typhoon ravages Filipino ministry—Philippines

In the aftermath of Typhoon Haiyan (Yolanda), the storm that struck the Philippines in November 2013 and left massive destruction in its wake, God's Word was needed more than ever. During a recent Bible outreach effort in one of the hard-hit areas, another destructive storm, Typhoon Ramassun, struck the Philippines. Despite this major setback, our team of committed Christians is continuing to share God's Word with their communities. Pray that typhoon battered Filipinos will find peace in God’s Word as they rebuild their lives, homes and churches following this recent round of destruction.

Recovering drug addicts find strength in God’s Word —Mexico

Pray for the restoration of broken lives of young people lost in drug addiction. Bible League reaches past addressing the surface of substance addiction and deals with root issues of character and behavior that so often contribute to addiction. Many who pass through the ministry’s program leave with a relationship with Jesus that not only breaks the bonds of addiction but also brings wholeness to their lives. Ask God to show His great favor so that more and more lives are changed forever through the power of the Spirit speaking through the Word of God.
